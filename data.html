Dictionary Data Sources

Attribution and download links for the dictionaries that power MoaVoca's word lookups.

MoaVoca builds its word lookup feature on top of several openly-licensed dictionary datasets, all distributed under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA). Per the ShareAlike clause, the underlying data remains freely redistributable from the original publishers — the links below take you directly to the upstream sources.

How we use this data

  • Dictionary entries are imported with minimal modification — primarily Unicode normalization and indexing for fast lookup.
  • Headword, part-of-speech, definitions, and pronunciation fields preserve the original wording from the upstream source.
  • AI-generated example sentences and contextual translations produced by our internal pipeline are derivative works of the input word only, not of the dictionary definition itself, and are attributed to MoaVoca rather than to the upstream dictionary.
License compliance. MoaVoca's use of these sources satisfies the BY (attribution) clause via this page and the in-app credits at Settings → Open Source Licenses → Dictionary Data Sources. The SA (ShareAlike) clause is satisfied by directing redistribution to the upstream publishers, who maintain the canonical CC BY-SA datasets in current form.
Need our processed copy?
Researchers, developers, or fellow open-source projects who would like a snapshot of MoaVoca's import-normalized dataset (rather than the upstream original) may request one by emailing support@moavoca.com. We respond within 30 days as required by CC BY-SA 4.0 §6(b).
