MoaVoca ("the App") is operated by Funston, a sole proprietorship registered in the Republic of Korea. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it, in line with Korean PIPA, EU/UK GDPR, and the California CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address: collected when you register an account (optional). Used for authentication, security notifications, and account deletion confirmation.
  • Password: collected for email account authentication. Stored only as a one-way salted hash on Supabase Auth. We cannot view it.
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In identifier: when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a stable per-app user identifier plus, where you authorize it, your name and email. Apple users who opt into "Hide My Email" share an Apple-relayed address; that is treated like any other email.
  • Display name (nickname) and username: the name shown to other users in friend and sharing features. Set by you.
  • Avatar selection: an avatar chosen from a predefined set or auto-generated initials. Custom photo upload is not supported.
  • Language settings: native language, source language, target language.
  • Wordlist data: list names, saved words, AI-generated definitions, your study progress, review counts, next-review dates, and learning streak.
  • Shared wordlists (community): wordlists you publish so other users can view them. Public to all users.
  • Friend graph: friends you have added, accepted, blocked, sent requests to, or "poked".
  • Camera/Photos: images you capture or select for OCR word extraction are sent to OpenAI Vision for processing. The original images are not retained on our servers after processing; only the extracted text is stored as a wordlist.
  • Microphone/Voice: voice input is processed entirely on-device by your operating system's speech engine (iOS Speech / Android SpeechRecognizer). Raw audio is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Device push token: an Apple APNs or Google FCM token used to deliver friend, poke, and learning-reminder notifications.
  • Country and timezone: collected during onboarding for monthly usage limits and notification timing. Changeable once per month.
  • Device language: read once on first launch to set the initial UI language. Not transmitted to our servers.
  • API usage logs: per-call counts, response times, costs, and error categories, keyed by your user id, used for service operations and abuse prevention.
  • Subscription status: premium subscription state and the RevenueCat transaction identifier for the active entitlement. Card numbers are processed entirely by Apple or Google and never reach us or RevenueCat.
  • Diagnostic data: crash reports and performance traces (via Sentry) with personal identifiers redacted client-side.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android): only when you grant tracking consent. See section 6.

3. Where Data Is Stored

Wordlists, vocabulary cache, and learning progress are stored both on your device (SQLite + AsyncStorage / Keychain) and on our server (Supabase, US region) for cross-device sync. AI lookup results may be stored anonymously in a shared cache to reduce duplicate compute. Authentication tokens are stored on your device in iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.

All processors listed in section 4 are located outside the Republic of Korea, primarily in the United States. By using MoaVoca and creating an account, you are informed that:

  • Receiving parties: the processors listed in section 4.
  • Receiving country: primarily United States.
  • Time and method of transfer: continuously, by encrypted HTTPS/TLS, at the time the relevant feature is used (e.g., a word lookup transfers to OpenAI).
  • Categories: as detailed per processor in section 4.

You may decline overseas transfer of your personal data by not creating an account — the App's offline learning features remain usable without sign-up. If you have already created an account and want to withdraw consent, request account deletion as described in section 12.

6. Advertising Identifiers and Personalized Ads

MoaVoca is free to download and is supported by ads. When you grant tracking permission (Apple ATT prompt on iOS) and ad-personalization consent (Google UMP form for users in the EU / California), Google AdMob receives your advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and ad-interaction data to deliver personalized ads on our behalf. If you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads instead and core App functionality is unaffected. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time from your device's system settings. Funston is a joint controller with Google for ad-personalization purposes within the meaning of GDPR Art. 26.

7. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

MoaVoca plays pronunciations using Microsoft Azure Neural TTS. When you tap a pronunciation button, the word or sentence is sent through our edge function to Azure, which returns a short MP3. Cached MP3s are stored on Supabase Storage and reused for everyone — no user identifier is associated with cache rows.

8. Speech Recognition

Voice input uses your operating system's on-device speech engine (iOS Speech, Android SpeechRecognizer). Raw audio is not transmitted to or stored on our servers — only the transcribed text is used for the lookup.

9. Push Notifications

Daily learning reminders, weekly recaps, and per-wordlist reminders are scheduled locally on your device. Friend events (incoming requests, acceptances) and pokes are delivered from our server through Apple APNs / Google FCM using the device push token you've registered with us. You can disable notifications from your operating system settings at any time.

10. Public Content and Wordlist Sharing

When you publish a wordlist publicly, the wordlist's name, description, word selection, and your display name / username and avatar become visible to all other users. Shared wordlists are public information and may be downloaded by others. We may, without prior notice, hide or remove inappropriate content; users can flag content through the in-app reporting system, and we act on credible reports within 24 hours (EU DSA Art. 16). When we take down content you uploaded, we send you a notice with the reason (DSA Art. 17).

11. Friend System

Users you have added as friends may see your display name, avatar, learning statistics (if you enable "Stats public"), and shared wordlists. Friend relationships are formed by mutual consent and may be removed or blocked at any time. After blocking, the blocked user can no longer interact with you. Friend graph data is stored on Supabase and is not disclosed outside the friendship itself.

How to request account deletion

In-app: Settings → Account → Delete Account → confirm.

By email: send an email to support@moavoca.com from the address associated with your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted: email, OAuth identifiers, display name, username, avatar selection, wordlists, learning progress, friend graph, shared wordlists, push tokens, per-wordlist notification settings, and any pending content reports you submitted.

What is retained: payment- and tax-related transaction records are retained for the period required by applicable law (typically 5 years for VAT and income tax in Korea). Personal identifiers in those records are pseudonymized.
